I am a M.S. student in Computer Science at the [University of Southern California](https://www.usc.edu/) and a Visiting Student Researcher at [Stanford University](https://www.stanford.edu/).

My research focuses on trustworthy and human-centered AI, with interests in privacy auditing for large language models, diversity in language model generation, LLM persona, and medical AI agents. I am broadly interested in understanding how AI systems behave in real-world settings and building methods to make them more reliable, private, and aligned with human needs.

Previously, I received my B.Eng. in Geodesy and Geomatics Engineering from [Wuhan University](https://www.whu.edu.cn/). I was also a visiting student at [UC Berkeley](https://www.berkeley.edu/), where I worked on visual-capable chatbot systems based on instruction-tuned language models.

## Research Interests

- Trustworthy large language models
- Privacy auditing and in-context learning
- LLM diversity and persona
- Medical AI agents
- Human-centered AI
